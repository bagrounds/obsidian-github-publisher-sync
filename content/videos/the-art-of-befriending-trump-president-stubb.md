## 🤔 Evaluation  
  
- 🟢 President Stubb argues that the 2022 invasion of Ukraine was the primary catalyst for the new world order.  
- 🔵 In contrast, many American scholars, such as those published in Foreign Affairs by the Council on Foreign Relations, suggest the shift began earlier with the rise of China and the 2008 financial crisis.  
- 🟡 While Stubb emphasizes a triangle of power, the book The Jungle Grows Back by Robert Kagan from the Brookings Institution suggests we are returning to a more chaotic, pre-WWII style of multipolarity rather than a structured triangle.  
- ⚪ To better understand these shifts, one should explore the specific economic dependencies between the Global South and the Global East that might limit Western influence.  
  
## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🇫🇮 Q: How does Finland defend its territory against potential Russian aggression?  
🦁 A: Finland utilizes a total defense model including mandatory military service for all men, a reserve of one million trained citizens, and one of the largest artillery forces in Europe. \[[25:16](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ubZeguAk0fM&t=1516)]  
  
### 🇺🇳 Q: What are the specific proposals for reforming the United Nations Security Council?  
🏛️ A: The proposal involves doubling the permanent membership to ten seats by adding representatives from Latin America, Africa, and Asia while removing the veto power. \[[29:59](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ubZeguAk0fM&t=1799)]  
  
### 📐 Q: What is the triangle of power mentioned by Alexander Stubb?  
🌍 A: It is a framework consisting of the Global West (led by the US/Europe), the Global East (led by China/Russia), and the Global South (middle powers) who hold the balance of power. \[[15:14](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ubZeguAk0fM&t=914)]
