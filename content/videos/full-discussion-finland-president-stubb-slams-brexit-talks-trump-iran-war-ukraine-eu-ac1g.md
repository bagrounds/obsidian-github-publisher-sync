### 🧩 Q: What is the concept of flexible integration in the European Union?  
🧩 A: Flexible integration is a governance mechanism that allows groups of EU member states to move forward with specific policies or integration levels at different speeds or through different institutional arrangements rather than requiring total consensus for every action.
