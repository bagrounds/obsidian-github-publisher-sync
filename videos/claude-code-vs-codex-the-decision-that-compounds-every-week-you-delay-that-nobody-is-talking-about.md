## 🤔 Evaluation  
  
- ⚖️ The video prioritizes developer experience and architectural efficiency but omits a deep dive into data privacy legalities which are critical for enterprise adoption according to The AI Hierarchy of Needs by Cogent World.  
- 🔍 While the speaker highlights performance gains from tight integration, research from McKinsey and Company in The Economic Potential of Generative AI suggests that interoperability between tools may be more valuable for large organizations than deep lock in with a single vendor's philosophy.  
- 🗺️ Further exploration is needed regarding how these coding harnesses will specifically translate into non-technical fields like marketing or legal operations as mentioned at the end of the video.  
  
## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🧰 Q: What is an AI harness in the context of software development?  
  
🏗️ A: An AI harness is the structural layer of software that surrounds a large language model to manage its connection to files, tools, memory, and execution environments.  
  
### 💸 Q: Why is the choice of AI harness considered a long term financial risk?  
  
📉 A: Teams build complex workflows, custom scripts, and documentation around a specific harness's architecture, making the cost of switching tools far higher than the monthly subscription fee.  
  
### 🛡️ Q: How do Claude Code and Codex differ in their approach to computer security?  
  
🔒 A: Claude Code operates directly on a user's local machine with full terminal access while Codex typically runs tasks in isolated cloud containers to prevent unauthorized system changes.
