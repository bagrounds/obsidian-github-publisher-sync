# 🚫✍️🚀 No one is writing Project 2029. So I started.  
![No one is writing Project 2029. So I started.](https://youtu.be/uBnIVzCjbLM)  
  
## 🤖 AI Summary  
* 🏗️ Build the American Foundations Act to reconstruct the democratic and economic infrastructure dismantled over the last fifty years. \[[00:20](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uBnIVzCjbLM&t=20)]  
* 📈 Match the minimum wage to worker productivity levels seen in 1980 and tie future increases to inflation. \[[02:42](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uBnIVzCjbLM&t=162)]  
* 🍼 Mandate federal paid family leave starting at three months and scaling up to one year to match other developed nations. \[[02:52](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uBnIVzCjbLM&t=172)]  
* ⚖️ Tax corporations at progressively higher rates based on the ratio between executive compensation and average worker pay. \[[03:13](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uBnIVzCjbLM&t=193)]  
* 🏠 Ban corporate landlords and provide a twenty five thousand dollar tax credit to help first time home buyers with down payments. \[[03:55](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uBnIVzCjbLM&t=235)]  
* 🗳️ Standardize federal elections with four weeks of early voting, mail in ballots, and a national election day holiday. \[[04:18](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uBnIVzCjbLM&t=258)]  
* 🗺️ Eliminate partisan gerrymandering by using independent commissions and socioeconomic algorithms to draw competitive districts. \[[04:40](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uBnIVzCjbLM&t=280)]  
* 🗳️ Replace winner take all electoral systems with ranked choice voting and move toward a direct popular vote. \[[05:11](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uBnIVzCjbLM&t=311)]  
* 🛂 Create a streamlined six month path to citizenship for peaceful, working undocumented immigrants for under five thousand dollars. \[[05:51](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uBnIVzCjbLM&t=351)]  
* 🏥 Introduce a public healthcare option for individuals earning up to one hundred twenty five thousand dollars to lower private costs. \[[06:42](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uBnIVzCjbLM&t=402)]  
* 🛡️ Require explicit congressional authorization for offensive military actions and executive branch layoffs exceeding five percent. \[[08:01](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uBnIVzCjbLM&t=481)]  
  
## 🤔 Evaluation  
* ⚖️ The proposal for ranked choice voting is often cited by FairVote as a primary method to reduce polarization and increase candidate diversity.  
* 🏠 While banning corporate landlords aims to lower prices, a report by the Urban Institute titled The Role of Institutional Investors in the Single Family Rental Market suggests these entities own a smaller percentage of the total market than often perceived, suggesting supply is a larger issue.  
* 💵 The Economic Policy Institute in their report The Productivity Pay Gap frequently discusses the decoupling of wages and productivity mentioned in the video.  
* 🏛️ Topics to explore further include the specific constitutional hurdles of abolishing the Electoral College and the macroeconomic effects of aggressive CEO pay-ratio taxation.  
  
## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 📄 Q: What is the American Foundations Act described in this video?  
📄 A: It is a proposed legislative framework designed to restore worker rights, stabilize housing, and modernize the American electoral and healthcare systems. \[[00:20](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uBnIVzCjbLM&t=20)]  
  
### 💰 Q: How would the proposed corporate tax structure affect CEO pay?  
💰 A: The act suggests charging higher tax rates to companies with high pay gaps between executives and average workers to discourage excessive compensation. \[[03:13](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uBnIVzCjbLM&t=193)]  
  
### 🗳️ Q: What changes does the framework suggest for United States elections?  
🗳️ A: It proposes standardizing voting rules across all states, ending gerrymandering, and implementing ranked choice voting nationwide. \[[04:18](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uBnIVzCjbLM&t=258)]  
  
### 🏥 Q: How would the public health option work under this plan?  
🏥 A: It would offer a government funded insurance choice with low deductibles that is free for individuals making under one hundred twenty five thousand dollars. \[[06:46](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uBnIVzCjbLM&t=406)]
