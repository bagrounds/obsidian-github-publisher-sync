# 👨‍🏫📈💻 Scott Galloway  
**🧑‍🏫 Scott Galloway** is a prominent American **🎤 public speaker, 👨‍🏫 academic, ✍️ author, 🎙️ podcast host, and 🚀 serial entrepreneur**.  
  
### 🎓 Academia & Media Presence  
* 👨‍🏫 **Professor:** He is a 👨‍🏫 **Clinical Professor of Marketing** at the 🏛️ **New York University (NYU) Stern School of Business**, where he teaches Brand Strategy and Digital Marketing to MBA students.  
* 🎙️ **Podcasts:** He is well-known as the host of 🎧 **The Prof G Show** and the co-host of the popular podcast 🗣️ **Pivot** (with Kara Swisher).  
* ✍️ **Author:** He is a 🥇 **New York Times bestselling author** of books such as:  
    * 📚 *The Four: The Hidden DNA of Amazon, Apple, Facebook, and Google*  
    * 📚 *The Algebra of Happiness*  
    * 📚 *Post Corona: From Crisis to Opportunity*  
    * 📊 *Adrift: America in 100 Charts*  
    * 💰 *The Algebra of Wealth*  
    * 👨‍🦱 *Notes on Being a Man* (his most recent work focusing on young men's issues)  
  
### 💼 Entrepreneurship & Business  
* 🚀 **Founder:** He has founded several companies, including:  
    * 📈 **Prophet:** A global brand strategy consultancy.  
    * 💡 **L2:** A subscription business intelligence firm focusing on digital competence (acquired by Gartner).  
    * 🎁 **Red Envelope:** An e-commerce gift company.  
    * 🧑‍💼 **Section4 (now Section):** An executive education startup.  
* 🤝 **Board Service:** He has served on the boards of directors for companies like 📰 **The New York Times Company** and 👕 **Urban Outfitters**.  
  
In short, he is a highly visible figure known for his sharp, often provocative analysis of technology 📱, business strategy 🎯, and current economic/societal trends 🌍.
