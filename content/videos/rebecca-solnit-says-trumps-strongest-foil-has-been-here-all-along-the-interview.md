## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🕯️ Q: How does Rebecca Solnit define the relationship between hope and history?  
  
🕯️ A: Solnit argues that hope is a form of memory that recognizes how much the world has changed through past activism, which counters the amnesia that leads to modern despair.  
  
### 🔋 Q: What is the primary barrier to solving the climate crisis according to the video?  
  
🔋 A: The barriers are political and economic, specifically the influence of the fossil fuel industry, rather than a lack of viable clean energy technology.  
  
### 👥 Q: Who does Solnit believe is the most effective counter to Trumpism?  
  
👥 A: She identifies civil society and collective action, rather than a single political leader, as the most powerful force against authoritarian movements.
