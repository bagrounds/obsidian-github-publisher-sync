## 🤖💌 AI Poetry  
⚔️ The will of many bends like reeds,  
👥 A thousand voices, thousand deeds.  
👑 But in the hush of turning page,  
💡 One mind persists beyond the stage.  
  
🧭 The compass points where numbers thin,  
🔥 Where sacrifice must first begin.  
✨ The many dream, the few remain,  
🌟 To carry forth the weight of gain.
