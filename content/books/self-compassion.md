## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### ❓ Q: Is this just about being nice to yourself?  
  
A: 🤓 No. Neff distinguishes self-compassion from self-pity or self-indulgence—it's about honest, kind awareness of your struggles, not denial or excuse-making.  
  
### ❓ Q: How is this different from self-esteem?  
  
A: 🤓 Self-esteem depends on external validation and being "above average." Self-compassion works regardless of outcomes—you don't have to be special to deserve kindness.  
  
### ❓ Q: Are there practical exercises?  
  
A: 🤓 Yes. The book includes guided meditations, journaling prompts, and specific techniques for different situations.
