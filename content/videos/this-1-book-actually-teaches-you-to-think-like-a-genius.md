### 🏆 Diane Halpern's Genius Thinking  
  
#### 🧠 Metacognition: Thinking About Thinking  
  
* 👁️ **Monitor Thinking:** Interrupt "fluency" (confusing familiarity with understanding). \[[00:44](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=44)]  
* ⏸️ **The Pause:** Stop before accepting intuitive claims or headlines. \[[01:36](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=96)]  
* ❓ **Self-Interrogation:** Ask: "Can I explain why this is true? What evidence supports/refutes it?" \[[01:36](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=96)]  
  
#### 🖼️ Reframing: Breaking the Language Lens  
  
* 🔄 **Translate Frames:** Convert "gains" into "losses" (and vice versa) to neutralize emotional bias. \[[02:02](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=122)]  
* ⚖️ **Logical Equivalence:** Identify if two scenarios are mathematically identical despite different wording. \[[03:09](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=189)]  
* 🚫 **Ignore Wording:** Base decisions on objective outcomes, not linguistic "clothing." \[[03:42](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=222)]  
  
#### 🔍 Reasoning: Logic over Belief  
  
* 🧩 **Deductive vs. Inductive:** Separate the validity of an argument from the truth of its conclusion. \[[04:12](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=252)]  
* 🃏 **Wason Task Logic:** Focus on potential violators of a rule (disconfirmation) rather than confirming cases. \[[06:13](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=373)]  
* 🎭 **Structure Recognition:** Identify the underlying logical pattern in different real-world contexts. \[[06:13](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=373)]  
  
#### 🛠️ Argument Analysis & Reconstruction  
  
* 🏗️ **Identify Components:** Explicitly list conclusions, premises, and hidden assumptions. \[[06:38](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=398)]  
* 📝 **Weekly Drill:** Rewrite a provocative article as a clean logical structure. \[[07:04](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=424)]  
* 🌊 **Surface Assumptions:** Resolve disagreements by exposing unstated beliefs. \[[06:57](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=417)]  
  
#### 🔓 Actively Open-Minded Thinking (AOMT)  
  
* 🛡️ **Defeat Defensive Reasoning:** Stop searching for confirmation; search for truth. \[[07:35](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=455)]  
* 🧪 **Disconfirmation Test:** Ask: "What realistic evidence would genuinely change my mind?" \[[07:59](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=479)]  
* 🚩 **Red Flag:** Recognize that if no evidence can change your view, reasoning has stopped. \[[08:13](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=493)]  
  
#### ⚗️ Hypothesis Testing & Causality  
  
* 👻 **Illusory Correlation:** Avoid inferring causation from coincidence or vivid anecdotes. \[[08:30](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=510)]  
* 🧬 **Generate Alternatives:** Brainstorm at least three competing explanations for any outcome. \[[09:13](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=553)]  
* 💊 **Check Placebo/Timing:** Question if the most "vivid" story is actually the correct one. \[[09:22](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=562)]  
  
#### ⚖️ Rational Decision-Making  
  
* ⚓ **Sunk Cost Awareness:** Abandon failing projects regardless of past time/energy investment. \[[09:30](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=570)]  
* ⚰️ **Pre-Mortem Drill:** Imagine a decision failed 6 months from now; list every reason why. \[[10:07](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=607)]  
* 📉 **Risk Mitigation:** Surface hidden dangers before committing resources. \[[10:15](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=615)]  
  
#### 🎨 Problem Solving & Creativity  
  
* 🗺️ **Systematic Reframing:** Rewrite a stuck problem in three ways: \[[10:30](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=630)]  
* 🎯 **Change the Goal:** Shift from "how to do X" to "what is the minimum outcome needed?" \[[11:18](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=678)]  
* ⏳ **Change Constraints:** Ask "what if I only had 15 minutes/half the budget?" \[[11:38](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=698)]  
* 👤 **Change Point of View:** View the problem from the perspective of the person executing it. \[[12:01](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=721)]

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🧐 Q: What is the primary difference between familiarity and understanding?  
  
🧠 A: Familiarity is a feeling of fluency that occurs when information sounds right, whereas understanding requires the ability to explain evidence and identify what would disprove a claim \[[01:44](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=104)].  
  
### 📉 Q: How does framing affect public health or financial decisions?  
  
🖼️ A: People tend to avoid risks when options are framed as guaranteed gains but become risk-seeking when the exact same outcomes are framed as guaranteed losses \[[03:09](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=189)].  
  
### 🚩 Q: What is the most common sign that reasoning has stopped?  
  
🔓 A: Reasoning has stopped when an individual is unable to identify any realistic piece of evidence that would cause them to change their mind about a belief \[[08:13](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=493)].  
  
### 🕒 Q: Why do people continue to invest in failing projects?  
  
⚓ A: This occurs due to sunk cost reasoning, where emotional attachment to past effort overrides the logical assessment of future utility \[[09:51](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ccHeVpHC9RM&t=591)].
