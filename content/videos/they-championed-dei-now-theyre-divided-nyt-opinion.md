* ⚖️ While the speakers argue DEI harms meritocracy, the American Association of University Professors in their statement On the Importance of Diversity in Higher Education maintains that diversity is essential to the educational mission and does not inherently conflict with high standards.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🧐 Q: What is the difference between equality and equity in a professional setting?  
⭐ A: Equality refers to providing the same resources to everyone regardless of their starting point, while equity involves providing specific resources or removing systemic barriers to ensure everyone has a level playing field to reach the same opportunities.  
  
### 📉 Q: Does research definitively prove that diversity increases company profits?  
⭐ A: The linkage is highly debated; while some McKinsey studies suggest a positive correlation, other researchers have argued these studies cherry-pick data and that no clear causal link exists between identity-based diversity and increased profitability.  
  
### 🤐 Q: What is psychological safety and how does it relate to DEI?  
⭐ A: Psychological safety is the belief that one can speak up with ideas, questions, or mistakes without being punished; however, critics argue it has been misused to over-police language and prevent the rigorous challenging of ideas.  
  
### 🏛️ Q: Why are some former DEI officers now calling for the elimination of diversity offices?  
⭐ A: Critics argue these offices create unnecessary bureaucracy, promote performative gestures like land acknowledgments, and fail to integrate inclusion into the actual culture of the company, making initiatives fragile and temporary.
