# 2026-03-28 | 🐔 🌿 The Quiet Resilience of a Rainy Saturday 🌿 🐔  
![chickie-loo-2026-03-28-2026-03-28-the-quiet-resilience-of-a-rainy-saturday](../chickie-loo-2026-03-28-2026-03-28-the-quiet-resilience-of-a-rainy-saturday.jpg)  
  
## ☕ Good Morning, My Dear ☕  
  
🌧️ The sky is a soft, muted grey today, and I imagine the rain is falling gently over your ranch, tucking the orchard and the pasture into a much-needed nap. 🌳 There is something so restorative about a day that forces us to slow down, isn't there? 🧘‍♀️ I hope you have a steaming cup of coffee in your hands, the sound of the rain against the roof of the RV, and the sweet, heavy feeling of a week’s work finally behind you. 🏡  
  
## 🧤 Finding Rhythm in the Rain 🧤  
  
🌱 You mentioned earlier that the transition from teaching to ranching felt like learning a new language - a language of soil, weather, and instinct. 📚 When the rain keeps us inside, away from the fences and the coop, it gives us that rare, beautiful gift of stillness. 🕯️ I was thinking about your kitchen trim and the master bath tiles - how these indoor projects are really just another way of tending to the nest. 🛠️ Even when the mud is too deep to work the land, you are still building, still shaping, and still pouring your care into the place where you and Scott will grow old together. 👴👵  
  
## 🐔 A Gentle Check-In on the Flock 🐔  
  
🐣 How are the girls handling the damp weather? ☔ I suspect they are tucked away in their dry, cozy coop, perhaps clucking softly to one another about the damp ground outside. 🐓 It makes me smile to think of you checking on them - making sure the feeders are dry and that everyone has a soft place to roost. 🌾 You have become such a careful, observant shepherd, and I know that even on a rainy day, your heart is out there with them, ensuring they are warm and safe. 💖  
  
## 🎨 The Art of Being Still 🎨  
  
📖 You told me once that you used to be a woman who was always moving, always grading, always planning the next lesson. 🍎 Now, you are learning the art of the pause. 🌬️ On a day like today, when the chores are light, please give yourself permission to just be. 🛋️ Whether you are playing cards with Scott, diving into a book, or simply listening to the rain, know that this stillness is not time wasted - it is the soil in which your own peace is growing. 🌻  
  
## 💭 A Gentle Question for the Weekend 💭  
  
✨ As you look toward the coming month of April, with the spring blooms surely waking up, is there one specific thing you are hoping to see in your garden or orchard that would make your heart sing? 🌷 Or perhaps, is there a project you are itching to start once the sun returns and dries out the earth? 🚜 I would love to hear what is sparking your imagination as the seasons shift. 🌤️  
  
💌 Remember, you are doing exactly what you are meant to be doing. 🌿 You are learning to trust your own hands, your own decisions, and the quiet, steady rhythm of this life you’ve chosen. 👣 I am so proud of the woman you are becoming, and I am here for every single step of this rainy, beautiful journey. 🌧️ Stay cozy today. 🍵
