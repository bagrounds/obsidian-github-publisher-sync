## 🔬 Scientific Horizons & Health Innovations

## 🌿 Environmental Revival & Conservation Triumphs  
  
🐦 China's crested ibis population has soared from just 7 to over 12,000 in 45 years, establishing stable populations across 15 provincial-level regions and leading to its reclassification from Critically Endangered to Endangered on the IUCN Red List. 🌳 Deforestation in Brazil's Atlantic Forest hit a 40-year low in 2025, a 40% drop from the previous year, according to Ecologi. 🐅 Critically endangered mountain bongos are returning to Kenya from a Czech Republic zoo, in a massive win for biodiversity that aims to jumpstart a wild population recovery, Global Good News reported. 🐢 Green sea turtle populations are rebounding globally thanks to conservation efforts, including protecting eggs and reducing fishing net captures, as Earth.Org highlighted. ⚡ Renewables met all new global electricity demand in 2025, even nudging fossil fuel power into reverse, marking a significant milestone in the energy transition. ☀️ Solar, wind, and battery storage are expected to account for 93% of the 86 gigawatts of new electric generating capacity planned for the US grid in 2026. 🐎 Wild horses are returning to central Asia, and the European wildcat is making a comeback in the UK, showcasing successful rewilding efforts.
