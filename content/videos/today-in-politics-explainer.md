## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🗺️ Q: How has the conflict in Iran affected the Straight of Hormuz?  
🚢 A: Iran has effectively closed or threatened the straight, where twenty percent of the world's oil and thirty percent of fertilizer components travel, by making the passage uninsurable for global shipping.  
  
### 📜 Q: What is the significance of the Fourth Amendment in current legislative battles?  
⚖️ A: The Fourth Amendment protects against unreasonable searches and seizures, and it is the focal point of opposition to government surveillance programs like FISA and unauthorized federal raids.  
  
### 🛡️ Q: What is Temporary Protected Status and why is it currently a political issue?  
🇭🇹 A: It is a legal status for immigrants from countries facing disasters or war; it is a point of contention as some officials try to end it for groups like Haitians despite ongoing instability in their home countries.
