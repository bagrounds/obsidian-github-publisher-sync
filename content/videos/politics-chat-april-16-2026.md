## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🗳️ Q: Why is a two party system essential for a functioning democracy?  
✅ A: It ensures a loyal opposition that provides oversight of those in power and prevents corruption by offering a peaceful way to change leadership through elections.  
  
### 🇭🇺 Q: How did Peter Magyar change the political strategy in Hungary?  
✅ A: He treated the ruling party as an illegitimate group that staged a coup, vowing to nationalize assets given to cronies and restore independent journalism.  
  
### 🌊 Q: What is the environmental risk of copper sulfide mining in Minnesota?  
✅ A: Unlike traditional mining, sulfide mining creates sulfuric acid when waste rock meets water, potentially turning pristine boundary waters into toxic waste sites.  
  
### ⚖️ Q: How has antitrust law changed since the Reagan administration?  
✅ A: It shifted to focus only on whether mergers lower short-term prices, ignoring the broader impacts on workers, local communities, and the environment.
