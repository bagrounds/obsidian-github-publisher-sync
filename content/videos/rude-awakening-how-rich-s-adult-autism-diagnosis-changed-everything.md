## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🧐 Q: What is the significance of getting an autism diagnosis as an adult?  
  
😊 A: An adult diagnosis can offer profound self-compassion and clarity, explaining a lifetime of feeling different and allowing for better communication of personal boundaries.  
  
### 🎭 Q: Does behavior change after receiving an autism diagnosis?  
  
😊 A: Many people experience a grand unmasking where they stop trying to appear neurotypical and become more direct about their sensory or social needs.  
  
### 🏢 Q: How does autism affect workplace performance and social interactions?  
  
😊 A: Autistic individuals may require more time to reflect on information and can be perceived as overly direct or rude due to a lack of social softening.
