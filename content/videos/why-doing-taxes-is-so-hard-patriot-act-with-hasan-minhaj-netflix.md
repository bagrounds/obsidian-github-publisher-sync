## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
### 🕵️ Q: What are dark patterns in software design?  
🕵️ A: Dark patterns are user interface designs specifically crafted to trick users into doing things they did not intend to do, such as purchasing unnecessary upgrades or subscriptions.  
  
### 💰 Q: Who is eligible to file their taxes for free in the United States?  
💰 A: Generally, taxpayers with an adjusted gross income of seventy nine thousand dollars or less are eligible for the IRS Free File program, though many are steered toward paid versions by commercial software.  
  
### 📮 Q: How does a return free filing system work?  
📮 A: In a return free system, the tax authority uses information already reported by employers and banks to prepare a tax return for the citizen, who then only needs to approve or correct the document.
