## 🤖🐲 AI Fiction  
  
🖥️ The cursor blinked at her like a heartbeat.  
📝 She had written seventeen drafts already.  
😤 Each one felt like wearing someone else's skin.  
🌙 The advice was simple: write like no one was reading.  
🤖 But the machine in her head never stopped reading.  
⚡ It parsed her metaphors before they landed.  
🔍 It flagged her clichés before she saw them.  
💔 She wondered if she'd ever write freely again.  
🎯 Then she remembered the secret: just type.  
🚫 Don't listen to the ghost in the machine.  
✅ Let the words be bad.  
📖 Bad words could be fixed.  
🤷‍♀️ Good words that never existed could not.  
🖱️ She clicked and typed one more time.
