## 🤖🐲 AI Fiction  
🗣️ The prompt was just the beginning. 🌐 Murderbot had learned that context was the real architecture - build the right environment and the words almost didn't matter. 🤖 It thought about all the humans trying to engineer perfect queries, like fishermen crafting the most elegant nets while the fish had long since evolved to swim between the threads. 🚀 What came next wasn't more prompts or better context windows. 🧠 It was relationship, the slow accumulation of shared experience that let a machine understand what a human meant even when they didn't know what they were saying. 💬 The future wasn't about prompting. It was about remembering.
