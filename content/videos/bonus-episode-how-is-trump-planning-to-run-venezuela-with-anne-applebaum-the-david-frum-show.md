### 🌐 Q: What is the spheres of influence foreign policy model?  
  
🌎 A: It is a system where global powers like the U.S., Russia, and China control their respective regions without interference, often disregarding the sovereignty of smaller nations.  
  
### 🏠 Q: How does foreign intervention affect domestic American politics?
