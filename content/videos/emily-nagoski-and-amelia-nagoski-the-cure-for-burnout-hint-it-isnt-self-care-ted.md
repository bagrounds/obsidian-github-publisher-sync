## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### ❓ Q: What is the main difference between stress and stressors?  
  
🟢 A: Stressors are external triggers like work or finances, while stress is the internal physiological response your body has to those triggers.  
  
### ❓ Q: How do you complete a stress cycle without leaving your job?  
  
🟢 A: You can complete the cycle through physical movement, deep breathing, positive social interaction, or creative expression to tell your body it is no longer in danger.  
  
### ❓ Q: Why is self-care alone often insufficient to cure burnout?  
  
🟢 A: Burnout is often a result of systemic issues and emotional exhaustion that require community support and human connection rather than just isolated relaxation.
