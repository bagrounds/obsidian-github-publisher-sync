### 💰 Q: What is the estimated additional cost for the Pentagon to continue the conflict?  
💰 A: The Pentagon indicates an additional two hundred billion dollars may be required to sustain ongoing military operations \[[00:35](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cVL_oFDlKyQ&t=35)].  
  
### 🎯 Q: What specific military goals have been achieved according to analysts?  
🎯 A: Forces have decapitated the regime, destroyed weapons factories, and significantly reduced the capabilities of proxy groups like Hamas and Hezbollah \[[03:42](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cVL_oFDlKyQ&t=222)].  
  
### 🗺️ Q: Why is the Strait of Hormuz a critical point of contention in this war?
