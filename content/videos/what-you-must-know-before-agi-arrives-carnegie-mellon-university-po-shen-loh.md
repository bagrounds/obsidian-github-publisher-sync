# 🤖🧠🚀 What you must know before AGI arrives | Carnegie Mellon University Po-Shen Loh  
![What you must know before AGI arrives | Carnegie Mellon University Po-Shen Loh](https://youtu.be/ivVPJhYM8Ng)  
  
## 🤖 AI Summary  
  
* 🧠 Humans will soon no longer be the most capable species on the planet as artificial intelligence advances.  
* 🧩 The most critical skill for the future is the ability to synthesize original ideas and think logically from scratch.  
* 🎨 AI creativity can already surpass human output, demonstrated by Google AI solving four of six International Math Olympiad problems.  
* 🚗 Using AI for school writing assignments is like driving a car for exercise; it results in zero mental fitness for the student.  
* 📏 Education must shift from learning how to do homework to learning how to grade and evaluate homework.  
* 🤝 Authentically caring about other humans is the only unique advantage remaining for human intelligence.  
* 📉 Modern test preparation and cramming industries are harmful because they rob students of the opportunity to invent.  
* 😊 Success correlates with the addictive pursuit of making other people happy and creating value for them.  
* 🎭 Effective education requires a live human experience with experts who possess both deep knowledge and friendly communication skills.  
* 🗺️ Simulating the world through empathy allows entrepreneurs to visualize problems through the eyes of others to find solutions.  
* 📱 Constant entertainment through devices like iPads has decreased the general human interest in deep concentration and thinking.  
* 🕵️ Without the ability to think critically, humans become easy to deceive by those with hidden agendas.  
* ⚖️ AI tools inherently possess the biases of their creators, making it essential to consume information from diverse, opposing viewpoints.  
  
## 🤔 Evaluation  
  
* ⚖️ While the speaker emphasizes human synthesis, the report AI Index Report 2024 by Stanford University Institute for Human-Centered AI notes that AI is already surpassing human performance on many benchmarks, including image classification and English understanding.  
* 🏫 The shift away from rote memorization toward critical thinking is a common theme in modern pedagogy, yet the World Development Report 2018 by The World Bank highlights a global learning crisis where many students fail to acquire basic foundational skills even before reaching complex synthesis.  
* 🌐 To gain a better understanding of these topics, one should explore the psychological impact of algorithmic bias on child development and the economic viability of non-scalable human-centric education models.  
  
## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🤖 Q: How does artificial intelligence affect the value of a university degree?  
  
🤖 A: University degrees may become less relevant if they focus on routine tasks, making the ability to synthesize original ideas and think autonomously the most important asset for a student.  
  
### ✍️ Q: Why is using AI for school writing assignments considered harmful?  
  
✍️ A: Writing is a form of mental exercise that develops logical thinking; outsourcing it to AI prevents the brain from becoming mentally fit, leading to dependency and a lack of reasoning skills.  
  
### 🧮 Q: What is the most effective way to learn mathematics in an AI dominated world?  
  
🧮 Q: Students should focus on solving original problems they have never seen before to practice mental flexibility rather than memorizing solutions for standardized tests.  
  
### 🤝 Q: What will be the primary reason for human employment in the future?  
  
🤝 A: People will be employed based on their ability to create value for others, their vibe, and their capacity to be a good partner in a team, as AI will handle most technical tasks.
