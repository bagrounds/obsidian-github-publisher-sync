## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### ❓ Q: Why is Trump's messaging on the Iran war considered shifting?  
📢 A: The administration provides conflicting signals on whether the U.S. is escalating or de-escalating while focusing on specific rescue successes instead of long-term strategic goals. \[[00:34](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdmguGOt398&t=34)]  
  
### ❓ Q: How are rising gas prices affecting the current political climate?  
📢 A: Gas prices over $4 a gallon create an affordability crisis that negates previous promises of cheap energy and weakens the president's core economic platform. \[[04:48](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tdmguGOt398&t=288)]  
  
### ❓ Q: What are the primary trade-offs in the proposed federal budget?
