## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### ❓ Q: Why did George Conway change his political party affiliation from Republican to Democrat?  
  
🔍 A: Conway switched his registration to the Democratic Party in December 2025 because he views the modern Republican Party as a cult that has abandoned the rule of law and conservative principles in favor of total submission to Donald Trump \[[02:25](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kV-qrG-ODpE&t=145)], \[[08:15](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kV-qrG-ODpE&t=495)].  
  
### ❓ Q: What is the primary focus of George Conway’s congressional campaign?  
  
🔍 A: His campaign aims to implement systemic safeguards against what he describes as an autocracy, with a central focus on using congressional power to demand accountability, including through the mechanism of impeachment, to prevent the government from functioning as a personal enterprise for the president \[[10:50](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kV-qrG-ODpE&t=650)], \[[11:47](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kV-qrG-ODpE&t=707)].  
  
### ❓ Q: How does George Conway explain the sustained support for Donald Trump?  
  
🔍 A: He suggests that Trump exploits deep-seated resentment and provides a permission structure for people to indulge in their worst impulses, while also noting that once supporters commit to his narrative, they often find it emotionally difficult to admit they were wrong because it requires confronting their own choices \[[18:25](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kV-qrG-ODpE&t=1105)], \[[20:26](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kV-qrG-ODpE&t=1226)], \[[23:25](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kV-qrG-ODpE&t=1405)].
