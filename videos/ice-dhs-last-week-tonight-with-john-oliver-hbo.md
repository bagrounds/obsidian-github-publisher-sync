## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🏢 Q: What is the primary purpose of the Department of Homeland Security?  
  
🏢 A: DHS was created to coordinate federal efforts to prevent terrorist attacks and manage national emergencies, though it now oversees a massive array of tasks including immigration enforcement and cybersecurity \[[05:08]()].  
  
### 💸 Q: How much funding does ICE currently receive compared to other agencies?  
  
💸 A: ICE has been granted a $75 billion increase, tripling its budget and making it wealthier than the entire Canadian military and the highest-funded federal law enforcement body in the US \[[15:14]()].  
  
### 🌪️ Q: Why is there concern about FEMA's ability to respond to disasters?  
  
🌪️ A: FEMA has lost a third of its staff and faces severe delays in funding approvals because the current Secretary requires a personal sign-off on any grant over $100,000 \[[25:20]()].
