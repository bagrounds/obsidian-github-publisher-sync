### ❓ Q: What are the primary themes explored in Children of Memory?  
A: 🤓 Children of Memory centers on the definition of personhood, the impact of isolation on small communities, and the subjective nature of truth.  
  
### ❓ Q: Is the tone of Children of Memory similar to its predecessors?
