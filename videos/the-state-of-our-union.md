## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🏛️ Q: How does the Supreme Court ruling on tariffs affect executive power?  
  
🏛️ A: The court reaffirmed that the power to levy taxes and tariffs resides with Congress, limiting the president’s ability to use emergency laws for economic policy.  
  
### 🌐 Q: What is the concept of variable geometry in international diplomacy?  
  
🌐 A: It is a system where middle-power countries form ad hoc coalitions to solve specific issues like trade or climate without relying on traditional superpowers.  
  
### 🛡️ Q: What are the primary concerns regarding current US military and border spending?  
  
🛡️ A: Critics argue that the rapid influx of billions exceeds the agencies' capacity to spend effectively, leading to waste and potential corruption in private contracts.
