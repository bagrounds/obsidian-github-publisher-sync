### 💡 Core Philosophy: Trump's Immigration Agenda  
* 🚫 Zero-Sum View: Immigration as a detriment to America.  
* 🚨 National Crisis Framing: Elevated immigration to a national crisis, fueled by fear-mongering (the caravan).  
* ⚔️ Political Utility: Used immigration to attack opponents, distract from negative news.  
* 👑 Executive Branch Power: Immigration policy predominantly driven by presidential proclamations, executive orders, departmental guidance.  
  
### 🔑 Key Policies & Actions  
* 🧱 Border Wall: Central obsession, conceived almost accidentally, became an operational, legislative, and legal quagmire.  
* 🛑 Asylum Restrictions: Blocked asylum-seekers and refugees, severely diminished humanitarian protections.  
    * 🚫 Closed Southern Border to asylum seekers.  
    * 🇲🇽 Reinstated Remain in Mexico policy.  
    * 📱 Suspended CBP One mobile application for appointments.  
* 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦💔 Family Separations: Intentional policy, driven by anti-immigration officials.  
* ✈️ Mass Deportations: Expanded efforts, increased ICE arrests, though overall deportations in first term remained below Obama levels.  
    * ⚡ Sought expansion of expedited removal nationwide.  
* 🚧 Legal Immigration Barriers: Imposed travel bans, curtailed entry for specific nationalities, created procedural and arbitrary barriers.  
* ⚖️ DACA & TPS: Attempted to strip discretionary protections from DACA recipients and Temporary Protected Status holders, largely stymied in court.  
* ❓ Citizenship Question: Attempted to add a citizenship question to the U.S. census.  
* 🛡️ Targeting Sanctuary Jurisdictions: Used punitive measures and threats against sanctuary cities/states.  
* 👶 Birthright Citizenship Challenge: Attempted to revoke citizenship for some U.S.-born children of immigrants via executive order, challenging the 14th Amendment.

* 🌪️ Chaos & Disorganization: White House decision-making marked by gut instinct, disorganization, paranoia, and feuding staff.  
* 🤔 Amoral Approach: Trump's choices characterized as more amoral than driven by hatred, focused on numbers making him look bad.
