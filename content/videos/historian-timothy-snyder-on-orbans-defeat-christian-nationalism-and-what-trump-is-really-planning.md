### 🇭🇺 Q: How did the Hungarian opposition defeat Victor Orban in 2026?  
🗳️ A: The opposition utilized independent media to expose corruption, engaged in two years of constant grassroots campaigning, and connected the government's abuse of power directly to the daily economic struggles of the citizens.  
  
### 🛡️ Q: Why is public protest considered a form of political deterrence?  
💪 A: Large scale mobilization demonstrates a capacity for resistance that makes it difficult for a regime to ignore election results or successfully carry out a coup without facing massive social backlash.  
  
### ⛪ Q: What is the relationship between Christian nationalism and authoritarianism?  
👑 A: It provides a metaphysical framework that places a leader's will above investigation or law, often leading to self deification where the leader claims a mystical connection to the people.
