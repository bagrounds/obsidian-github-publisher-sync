# 2026-02-12 | 🧮 Mathematical 💪 Strength 📚  
## 🤖💌 AI Poetry  
👶 They enter with the counting song,  
🧠 A knowing that has been all along.  
✨ Before the symbols, signs, or rules,  
💫 They carry mathematics like a jewel.  
  
➕ Addition is not taught but found,  
➖ Subtraction hides in every sound.  
🌟 The strength of few who guide the way,  
🧮 Shows what children already say.
