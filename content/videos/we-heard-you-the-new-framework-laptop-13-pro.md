* 💻 Framework introduces the Laptop 13 Pro as a ground-up redesign focusing on professional-grade performance and efficiency.  
* 🔋 Battery life sees a massive improvement, reaching over 20 hours of 4K streaming, which surpasses the MacBook Pro M5 in specific use cases.  
* ⚡ The system features Intel Core Ultra Series 3 processors, known as Panther Lake, utilizing the highly efficient 18A process node.

* 🖱️ A haptic touchpad is integrated to provide a premium tactile experience comparable to the industry gold standard.  
* 🐧 Linux compatibility is prioritized from the start with Ubuntu-certified pre-built options and upstream firmware support.  
* 🔄 Backward compatibility remains a core tenet, allowing users to install the new Pro mainboard into original Framework 13 chassis.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🔋 Q: How does the Framework Laptop 13 Pro achieve 20 hours of battery life?  
🔋 A: The laptop utilizes a new 74Wh high-density battery combined with the extreme power efficiency of Intel Core Ultra Series 3 Panther Lake processors and modular LPCAM2 memory.  
  
### 🐧 Q: Is the Framework Laptop 13 Pro compatible with Linux out of the box?  
🐧 A: Yes, it is the first Framework product to be Ubuntu certified, featuring components specifically selected for compatibility and pre-loaded Ubuntu options for business and individual users.  
  
### 🛠️ Q: Can I upgrade my existing Framework Laptop 13 with the new Pro internals?  
🛠️ A: The new Pro mainboard maintains the same form factor, allowing owners of any previous Framework 13 generation to drop the new board and battery into their current chassis.  
  
### 🖥️ Q: What makes the new Pro display different from previous generations?  
🖥️ A: It is a fully custom 2.8K panel with a 3:2 aspect ratio that increases brightness to 700 nits, adds 120Hz variable refresh rates, and introduces a matte touch screen without extra glass layers.
