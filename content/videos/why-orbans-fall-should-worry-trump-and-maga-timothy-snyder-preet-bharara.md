## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🗳️ Q: How can an opposition party win in a rigged electoral system?  
📦 A: Success requires massive voter mobilization and a focus on connecting government corruption to the daily economic struggles and loss of affordability faced by citizens.  
  
### 🛑 Q: What does it mean to not obey in advance?  
📦 A: It is the practice of maintaining one's own standards of professional and moral conduct rather than preemptively changing behavior to please a potential or current authoritarian leader.  
  
### 📱 Q: How do digital algorithms impact democratic participation?  
📦 A: Algorithms are designed to capture attention by triggering impulsive emotions like fear and anger, which reduces the capacity for the thoughtful reflection and empathy required for a healthy democracy.
