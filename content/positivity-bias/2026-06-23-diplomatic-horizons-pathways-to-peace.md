## 🕊️ Diplomatic Horizons & Pathways to Peace

## 🌿 Environmental Resilience & Conservation Wins  
  
🌊 More than 10% of the global ocean is now officially under protection, marking a historic milestone in biodiversity conservation and supporting fishing communities, Rare magazine reported. 🤝 The Coastal 500 network has surpassed its goal, uniting over 500 local government leaders across eight countries dedicated to protecting coastal ecosystems, Rare stated. 🌳 A major initiative called ARPA Comunidades is securing 60 million acres of Amazon floodplain forest by empowering Indigenous Peoples and local communities to manage and patrol it, according to Rare. 💰 Donor countries have pledged an initial $3.9 billion to the Global Environment Facility for its ninth replenishment cycle (2026-2030), providing a significant infusion of funds for global biodiversity and climate targets, Rare highlighted. 🐠 New research provides the first baseline population estimate of the critically endangered widenose guitarfish along India's coast, identifying key nearshore habitats, Future For Nature reported. 🐢 Two two-toed sloth species have been added to CITES Appendix II, marking an important milestone for sloth conservation, according to Future For Nature. 🌍 Regenerative agriculture offers a pathway to restore soil health, biodiversity, and climate resilience in Southeast Asia through practices like crop diversification, intercropping, and biofertilizers, as detailed by the ISEAS – Yusof Ishak Institute. ♻️ Pennsylvania has modernized its e-waste law, expanding access to convenient and responsible recycling for end-of-life electronics, thereby reducing environmental impact and supporting a circular economy, according to a YouTube broadcast from the Environmental & Natural Resource Protection Committee. 🌳 World Rainforest Day was observed on June 22, promoting awareness and action for these vital ecosystems, Reader's Digest noted.
