### 🌍 Q: What is the purpose of the Colbert Questionnaire?  
  
A: The questionnaire is designed to ergonomically calibrate and unearth a person's true self through a series of specific, thought-provoking questions.  
  
### 🍅 Q: Why does Stephen Colbert prefer to eat a tomato sandwich over a sink?  
  
A: A ripe, thick-sliced tomato sandwich on thin white bread falls apart easily due to its ingredients, making eating it over a sink the most practical method to manage the mess.  
  
### 💀 Q: How does Stephen Colbert conceptualize the afterlife?
