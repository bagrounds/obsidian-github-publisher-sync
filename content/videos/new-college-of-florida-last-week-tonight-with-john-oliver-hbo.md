## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### ❓ What was the primary motivation behind the takeover of New College of Florida?  
  
The conservative leadership, led by figures like Chris Rufo and Governor Ron DeSantis, sought to reorient the liberal arts institution toward conservative values and create a replicable model for transforming higher education in red states \[[01:23](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jFMc07F1UUU&t=83)], \[[04:13](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jFMc07F1UUU&t=253)].  
  
### ❓ How did the student experience change after the administration transition?  
  
Students faced the loss of faculty, the elimination of existing programs like gender studies, the removal of student resources, and the prioritization of newly recruited student athletes in housing and scholarships \[[12:20](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jFMc07F1UUU&t=740)], \[[13:23](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jFMc07F1UUU&t=803)], \[[22:08](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jFMc07F1UUU&t=1328)], \[[22:24](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jFMc07F1UUU&t=1344)].  
  
### ❓ Why was the recruitment of baseball players criticized?  
  
The institution recruited 70 baseball players despite having no existing baseball team or facilities, using significant state funding to provide merit-based scholarships that disproportionately favored these new recruits over existing students \[[21:11](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jFMc07F1UUU&t=1271)], \[[22:02](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jFMc07F1UUU&t=1322)].
