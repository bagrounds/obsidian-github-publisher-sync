## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### ❓ Q: Do I need to be born creative to benefit from this book?  
  
A: 🤓 No! Grant argues that originality is a learned skill, and provides practical strategies for generating, recognizing, and championing new ideas regardless of your starting point.  
  
### ❓ Q: Is this just another business innovation book?  
  
A: 🤓 While useful for business contexts, the principles apply broadly to anyone wanting to challenge the status quo in any domain, from parenting to politics to personal projects.  
  
### ❓ Q: What makes this different from other creativity books?  
  
A: 🤓 Grant focuses on the social and organizational dynamics of championing ideas, not just generating them, covering how to navigate resistance, build coalitions, and time your initiatives strategically.
