# 🐦📰🤣 Twitter: Last Week Tonight with John Oliver (HBO)  
![Twitter: Last Week Tonight with John Oliver (HBO)](https://youtu.be/p7ZG_xWYLzI)  
  
## 🤖 AI Summary  
* 📉 Twitter has undergone a massive transformation since Elon Musk purchased the site in October 2022, including cutting approximately 80% of the staff \[[01:11](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p7ZG_xWYLzI&t=71)].  
* 🔏 Musk replaced the traditional verification system with a paid model where anyone can buy a blue check for $8 a month, leading to immediate chaos and impersonation \[[03:01](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p7ZG_xWYLzI&t=181)].  
* 🛡️ Global trust and safety staff were slashed, and full-time content moderators were reduced by over 50%, weakening the platform's ability to handle harmful content \[[04:30](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p7ZG_xWYLzI&t=270)].  
* 🔓 Banned accounts belonging to far-right activists, white supremacists, and conspiracy theorists were reinstated under a general amnesty \[[05:05](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p7ZG_xWYLzI&t=305)].  
* 🗞️ The site is now an active liability during breaking news events, frequently circulating misinformation and digitally altered images \[[05:24](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p7ZG_xWYLzI&t=324)].  
* 💰 A new monetization system pays premium users based on engagement, incentivizing provocative, untrue, or brash content designed to egg people on \[[06:14](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p7ZG_xWYLzI&t=374)].  
* 🌍 Investigation into pro-Trump accounts revealed many are operated by users in Eastern Europe, Africa, and Pakistan rather than the United States \[[08:26](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p7ZG_xWYLzI&t=506)].  
* 📈 Algorithmic experiments suggest the platform feeds a glut of right-wing content even to neutral or left-leaning users \[[10:08](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p7ZG_xWYLzI&t=608)].  
* 🏛️ The United States government has become troublingly dependent on the platform, with officials openly taking work cues and opening investigations based on internet leads \[[14:03](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p7ZG_xWYLzI&t=843)].  
* 🚨 Misinformation on the platform has had lethal real-world consequences, such as fueling the 2024 UK riots following a knife attack in Southport \[[22:14](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p7ZG_xWYLzI&t=1334)].  
  
## 🤔 Evaluation  
* ⚖️ While the video focuses on the degradation of safety, The Battle for the Bird by Bloomberg journalist Kurt Wagner provides a detailed history of the internal corporate struggle and the financial realities leading to the sale.  
* 🔎 The claim regarding right-wing algorithmic bias is supported by a study titled Algorithmic Amplification of Politics on Twitter published by the Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences.  
* 🌐 For a deeper understanding of digital harm, explore the research provided by the Center for Countering Digital Hate regarding the rise of hate speech under new ownership.  
  
## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🧩 Q: How has the Twitter verification system changed under Elon Musk?  
🧩 A: The system shifted from a free identity-verification process for notable figures to a paid subscription service where anyone can purchase a blue checkmark for a monthly fee \[[03:01](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p7ZG_xWYLzI&t=181)].  
  
### 💸 Q: Can users make money directly from posting on Twitter?  
💸 A: Yes, premium subscribers are eligible for revenue sharing based on the number of replies, reposts, and likes their content receives from other premium users \[[06:14](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p7ZG_xWYLzI&t=374)].  
  
### 🛑 Q: What happened to the content moderation teams at Twitter?  
🛑 A: The global trust and safety staff was significantly reduced, and more than half of the full-time content moderators were laid off following the acquisition \[[04:30](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p7ZG_xWYLzI&t=270)].  
  
### 🇬🇧 Q: How did Twitter impact the 2024 civil unrest in the United Kingdom?  
🇬🇧 A: The platform served as an accelerant for the riots by spreading false claims about the identity of an attacker in Southport, reaching over 155 million impressions \[[24:07](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p7ZG_xWYLzI&t=1447)].
