## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### ❓ Q: How does the 25th Amendment work to remove a President?  
✅ A: Under Section 4, the Vice President and a majority of the Cabinet must submit a written declaration of presidential inability to Congress, immediately transferring power to the Vice President as Acting President \[[21:23](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3Z3l_ead5mc&t=1283)].  
  
### ❓ Q: What is the significance of the Strait of Hormuz in this conflict?  
✅ A: It is a vital global chokepoint through which over twenty percent of the world's oil and significant amounts of liquid natural gas and fertilizer pass; a closure threatens global economic stability \[[01:34](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3Z3l_ead5mc&t=94)].  
  
### ❓ Q: Why did Dwight Eisenhower support the 25th Amendment?  
✅ A: Eisenhower sought a clear process for the transfer of power due to his own health issues and his wartime observations of how mental fatigue can lead to fatal military errors \[[18:59](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3Z3l_ead5mc&t=1139)].
