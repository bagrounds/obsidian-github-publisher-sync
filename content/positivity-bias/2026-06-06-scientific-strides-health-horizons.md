## 🔬 Scientific Strides & Health Horizons  
  
💊 The National Institute for Health and Care Excellence (NICE) in the UK has approved four new treatments for NHS use, including a combination of encorafenib and binimetinib for adults with BRAF V600E mutation-positive non-small cell lung cancer. 💡 May also saw five notable oncology FDA approvals, featuring a first-in-class PROTAC for breast cancer and an immunotherapy combination for bladder cancer, expanding crucial treatment options. 💉 A breakthrough pill, daraxonrasib, has been shown to nearly double the survival time for patients with advanced pancreatic cancer in a 500-person trial, offering significant hope against this historically challenging disease. 🧠 Vanderbilt Health researchers have discovered that an investigational heart drug, ifetroban, may reduce the risk of metastasis in breast, lung, and other solid tumors. 🔬 Scientists have identified blood proteins that can predict lung cancer more than five years before diagnosis, with early evidence suggesting an existing anti-inflammatory drug could reduce risk.  
  
🧪 In a significant public health achievement, scientists have successfully tested an AI-designed universal coronavirus vaccine in humans for the first time, proving it safe and well-tolerated while generating broad immune responses. 🌌 Beyond medicine, researchers have uncovered unexpected quantum complexity within cobalt, a metal previously thought fully understood, opening new avenues for next-generation computing and spin-based devices. 🦎 In an intriguing biological discovery, fire salamanders, long-studied amphibians, have been found to biofluoresce, emitting a bluish-green glow under ultraviolet light. 🩹 A topical gel containing 4-aminopyridine (4-AP) has been developed to help burn wounds heal faster, achieving nearly complete closure in just 21 days. 🧠 A pioneering pilot clinical trial using spinal cord stimulation has shown promising improvements in arm function for individuals with chronic stroke. 💊 TearSolutions received FDA Orphan Drug and Fast Track Designations for Lacripep, an investigational synthetic peptide for neurotrophic keratitis, an unmet medical need. 💡 Rezolute announced positive interim data for its Phase 3 upLIFT study of ersodetug, reinforcing its potential as a universal treatment option for refractory hypoglycemia in tumor hyperinsulinism. 🔬 Exelixis' CABOMETYX demonstrated positive results in treating advanced neuroendocrine tumors (NET), showing significant improvements in progression-free survival.
