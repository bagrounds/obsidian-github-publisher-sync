## 🤖 AI Summary  
### 🧠 Core Philosophy  
* 😤 Emotional Immaturity: Parents prioritize own needs, lack empathy, react defensively.  
* 😔 Child's Role: Often becomes caretaker, seeks connection unsuccessfully, internalizes parental distress.  
* 🌟 Healing Goal: Reclaim authentic self, establish protective boundaries, foster emotional autonomy.  
### ✅ Actionable Steps  
* 🧐 **Identify Parent Types:** Recognize emotional, driven, passive, or rejecting parental styles to understand their impact.  
* 👀 **Observe, Don't Engage:** Witness parental behavior without internalizing or reacting emotionally, fostering detachment.  
* ✂️ **Emotional Detachment:** Disconnect from futile attempts to change parents, accepting their limitations.  
* 💖 **Self-Care & Validation:** Prioritize own feelings, needs; seek support from healthy sources to counter chronic self-doubt.  
* 🚫 **Establish Boundaries:**  
    * 🗣️ Verbal: Clearly and concisely state limits.  
    * 🚶‍♀️ Action-based: Limit contact, end conversations when boundaries are crossed.  
    * 🧠 Internal: Adjust expectations, understanding parents' capacity for change is limited.  
* 🦅 **Reclaim Autonomy:** Develop an internal sense of self, make independent choices, and cultivate self-awareness.  
* 💧 **Process Grief:** Acknowledge and mourn the loss of the desired, healthy parental relationship.  
* 💪 **Inner Adult:** Cultivate a strong, protective inner voice to guide and support oneself.
