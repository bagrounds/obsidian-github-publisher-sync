## 🤖 AI Summary

## 🤔 Evaluation  
  
* 🧪 The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention states two doses of MMR vaccine are 97 percent effective at preventing measles.  
* 🏥 According to the Mayo Clinic, measles can lead to severe complications like pneumonia or brain swelling in unvaccinated populations.  
* 🔍 Further investigation into the specific strain could determine if the university case links to the county outbreak.  
* 💉 Public health data from the Washington State Department of Health helps track regional vaccination rate trends.  
  
## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🧼 Q: What are the primary symptoms of a measles infection?  
  
🧼 A: Initial signs include high fever, cough, runny nose, and red watery eyes followed by a characteristic rash.  
  
### ⏳ Q: How long is the mandatory quarantine period after measles exposure?  
  
⏳ A: Health officials require a 21 day quarantine for non-immune individuals to ensure the virus does not spread.  
  
### 📍 Q: Where did the recent Snohomish County measles exposures take place?  
  
📍 A: Exposures happened at Serene Lake Elementary, Pathfinder Kindergarten Center, and Swedish Hospital in Mill Creek.
