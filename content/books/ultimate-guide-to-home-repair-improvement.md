### 💪 Core Repair & Improvement Principles  
* 💧 **Plumbing**:  
    * 🔎 Regular inspection for leaks, corrosion.  
    * 🚰 Prevent clogs: drain guards, avoid grease.  
    * 🌡️ Monitor water pressure.  
    * 🥶 Insulate pipes for winter.  
    * 🔥 Test water heater pressure relief annually.  
* 🔌 **Electrical**:  
    * 💡 Always turn off power at breaker.  
    * ⚡ Use insulated tools and GFCIs.  
    * 🧠 Understand basic concepts: voltage, current, resistance, grounding.  
    * 🔄 Replace like-for-like outlets/fixtures for basic tasks.  
* 🛠️ **General Maintenance**:  
    * 🏠 Regularly inspect structural elements.  
    * 🩹 Address small issues promptly to prevent escalation.  
    * 📄 Follow manufacturer instructions for all installations.

* 📜 **Permit Requirements**: Projects requiring permits and inspections (e.g., new circuits, panel upgrades).  
* 👨‍🔧 **Specialized Knowledge**: HVAC, handling aluminum wiring, projects beyond personal skill.
