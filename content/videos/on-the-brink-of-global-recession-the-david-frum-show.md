## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### ⛽ Q: How does a conflict in the Middle East affect US gas prices if the US is a net exporter?  
⛽ A: Oil is a global commodity, meaning shortages in Asia or Europe cause international bidding that raises prices for American consumers regardless of local production levels. \[[10:02](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BH0H4dgmmq0&t=602)]  
  
### 📉 Q: What is stagflation and why is it a risk now?  
📉 A: Stagflation occurs when the economy slows down while prices rise; it is triggered when central banks are forced to hike interest rates to fight inflation during an economic shock. \[[12:55](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BH0H4dgmmq0&t=775)]  
  
### 🚢 Q: Why are US allies seeking energy independence from America?  
🚢 A: Allies perceive recent US trade and foreign policies as capricious, leading them to view dependence on American resources as a strategic liability rather than a security guarantee. \[[33:58](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BH0H4dgmmq0&t=2038)]
