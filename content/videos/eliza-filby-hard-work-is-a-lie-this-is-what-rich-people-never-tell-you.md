### 👵 Q: What is the great wealth transfer?  
  
👴 A: It is an expected movement of over 100 trillion dollars from baby boomers to younger generations by 2048 \[[00:07](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nhvL48LgmT8&t=7)].  
  
### 🏦 Q: How does the Bank of Mom and Dad function?  
  
🏠 A: It refers to parents providing financial assistance for major life milestones like home down payments or university tuition \[[21:03](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nhvL48LgmT8&t=1263)].  
  
### 📉 Q: Why are millennials struggling more than their parents?  
  
💸 A: They face greater wealth inequality and higher costs for essentials like housing and education compared to previous generations \[[00:00](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nhvL48LgmT8&t=0)].
