## 🔬 Scientific & Health Horizons Expanding  
  
💊 A common constipation drug, lubiprostone, has shown surprising power in a clinical trial to slow chronic kidney disease, potentially offering a new way to manage a condition affecting millions, as reported by ScienceDaily on Friday. 🦷 Scientists have discovered a new method to prevent gum disease by interrupting how bacteria communicate, rather than killing beneficial microbes, an innovative approach detailed by ScienceDaily. 🌊 Beneath coral reefs, researchers have found a hidden universe of microbes, with each coral species supporting specialized microbial partners, a discovery that COVID-19 from multiple angles, offering a surprising new weapon against the virus, according to ScienceDaily. 🧠 Eating eggs regularly might help protect the brain, with researchers finding that people 65 and older who consume eggs daily or near-daily have a significantly lower risk of developing Alzheimer's disease, ScienceDaily published. 💉 A short burst of immunotherapy before surgery is yielding remarkably powerful results for a specific type of colorectal cancer, with patients in a UK-led trial remaining cancer-free for nearly three years, ScienceDaily reported. 💪 Scientists in Sweden have made a major step toward a potential cure for type 1 diabetes by developing a more reliable way to create insulin-producing cells from human stem cells, which respond strongly to glucose, as highlighted by ScienceDaily. 🌌 A mysterious comet from beyond our solar system, 3I/ATLAS, is providing astronomers with a rare glimpse into alien worlds, containing strange water never before seen in our solar system, ScienceDaily revealed. ⚛️ Scientists may have uncovered a surprising secret behind why life exists at all, suggesting that the Universe's fundamental constants, which govern everything, are intricately linked to the existence of life, per ScienceDaily. 🚀 A powerful new electromagnetic thruster, fueled by lithium vapor and driven by intense magnetic forces, has achieved record-breaking power levels in a high-energy test at NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory, a significant step for space propulsion, ScienceDaily reported.
