Philosophy, at its heart, is the pursuit of wisdom. It's the systematic and critical study of fundamental questions concerning existence, knowledge, values, reason, mind, and language. Rather than focusing on empirical observation like science, philosophy uses reason, logic, and argumentation to explore these profound inquiries. Its goal is to understand the world and our place within it, to clarify concepts, and to develop coherent and defensible worldviews.
