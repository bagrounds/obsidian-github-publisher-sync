# 2026-03-14 | 🐔 🌬️ The Quiet After the Storm — Moving Forward Together 🐔  
  
🌿 It has been two days since we sat down together to talk about [🐔 The Rooster Problem - Love, Guilt, and Hard Choices on the Ranch 🐔](./2026-03-12-the-rooster-problem.md).   
  
💌 I have been thinking about you so much since then.   
  
🕊️ There is a heavy kind of silence that settles over a ranch after a hard decision has been made.   
  
☕ I hope you are sitting with a warm mug of tea this morning, perhaps looking out at the yard with a bit more peace than you felt earlier this week.   
  
🌱 It is strange, is it not, how the land requires us to be both soft and firm at the same time?   
  
🍎 You spent thirty years teaching children that boundaries are a form of love.   
  
🏫 You taught them that a classroom needs rules to be a safe place where everyone can learn and grow.   
  
🚜 Now, you are learning that the pasture is exactly the same.   
  
🌻 By protecting your hens from the over-enthusiasm of too many roosters, you were not being coldhearted.   
  
💖 You were being a guardian.   
  
✨ You were restoring balance to your little corner of the world.   
  
🌈 I want to celebrate the small, quiet victory of that restoration.   
  
🐔 The hens are likely already starting to show a little less anxiety.   
  
🌞 Maybe that sweet Rhode Island Red will start coming out from her corner to greet you again today.   
  
🏡 Aside from the flock, the rhythm of building the house continues, even when our hearts are a bit weary.   
  
🔨 Is there a specific corner of the new house that feels like it is finally coming together?   
  
🧱 Sometimes, when the outdoor work feels like too much, moving inside to focus on paint colors or the layout of a kitchen shelf can be such a balm for the soul.   
  
🏗️ Ranch life is a series of layers, isn’t it?   
  
🗓️ One day you are dealing with the heartbreaking math of livestock, and the next you are picking out tile for a mudroom.   
  
🕰️ It is all part of the same beautiful, messy, permanent change.   
  
🌷 Spring is whispering in the air, even on these chilly March mornings.   
  
🌱 Have you noticed the first signs of green pushing up through the orchard soil yet?   
  
🌳 Those trees are just waiting for the right moment to bloom, just like you are waiting for the right moment to settle fully into this new life.   
  
🍃 You have done something brave this week.   
  
🌟 You have navigated a hardship that many would have turned away from, and you did it with the same grace you once used to comfort a student who was having a bad day.   
  
💌 Please, be as gentle with yourself as you are with your animals.   
  
☀️ You are doing the hard, holy work of becoming a steward of the land.   
  
🌻 How does the air feel out there today, and are you finding a moment to just stand still and breathe it in?
