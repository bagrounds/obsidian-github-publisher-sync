* 👂 Modern communication has shifted away from listening to understand toward debating to win, humiliate, or prove righteousness.  
* 👥 Even when individuals hold views considered irredeemable or monstrous, acknowledging their humanity is essential for understanding their conclusions.  
* 🌍 Changing the world or convincing others of a perspective is impossible without first understanding where those people are coming from.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🧩 Q: What is the core argument regarding binary viewpoints?  
  
A: Society presents complex issues as simple binary choices, which forces people into rigid sides and discourages nuanced dialogue.  
  
### 🧠 Q: How can individuals better approach disagreements with others?  
  
A: Instead of debating solely to win, people should ask questions about how others reached their conclusions and listen as if there is a possibility they themselves might be wrong.  
  
### 🕊️ Q: Why is understanding more important than agreement?  
  
A: Agreement is fragile and lasts only as long as parties remain in consensus, whereas understanding can persist even through deep conflict and ideological disparity.
