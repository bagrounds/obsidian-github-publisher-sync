## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🧐 Q: What is the significance of the speech and debate clause for members of Congress?  
  
🛡️ A: This constitutional provision protects legislators from being prosecuted or questioned in court for any speech or debate made during congressional proceedings, ensuring they can perform duties without executive interference.  
  
### 🏠 Q: Can ICE agents enter a private home without a warrant signed by a judge?  
  
⚖️ A: Under the Fourth Amendment, a judicial warrant based on probable cause is typically required to enter a home, though recent reports suggest internal memos have attempted to bypass this requirement for immigration agents.  
  
### 🗳️ Q: How did the fake electors scheme intended to function during the 2020 election?  
  
📋 A: Proponents organized unauthorized groups to sign documents claiming to be legitimate electors, hoping the Vice President would count them or use the dispute to throw the election to the House of Representatives.
