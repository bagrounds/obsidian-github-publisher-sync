## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 📅 Q: Why do most people fail their New Year goals so quickly?  
  
📅 A: Most people rely on temporary hype rather than writing down specific goals and creating a daily tactical plan to execute them.  
  
### ✍️ Q: What is the most important first step to achieving a goal?  
  
✍️ A: You must write the goal down to move it from a vague idea in your mind into a codified and measurable objective.  
  
### 🛑 Q: How should someone handle procrastination?  
  
🛑 A: Recognize that procrastination is an internal negotiation and use the mantra do it now to execute without hesitation.  
  
### 🔄 Q: What should I do if I miss a workout or break my diet?  
  
🔄 A: Avoid throwing the baby out with the bathwater and simply get back on the path immediately instead of waiting for tomorrow.
