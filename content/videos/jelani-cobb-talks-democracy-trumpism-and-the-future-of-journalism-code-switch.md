## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🧐 Q: What is the main thesis of Jelani Cobb's new book, Three or More Is a Riot: Notes on How We Got Here: 2012-2025?  
  
🤖 A: The book Three or More Is a Riot tracks a 13-year trajectory from the Ferguson protests to the second Trump term to show how patterns of militarization and political reactionism led to the current state of American democracy.  
  
### 👮 Q: Why does Cobb criticize the use of the National Guard in cities?  
  
🤖 A: He argues that crime reduction is not a valid legal or democratic justification for domestic military deployment and that such actions normalize the militarization of civilian life.  
  
### 📰 Q: What is the role of journalism in a period of political instability?  
  
🤖 A: Journalism must act as a moral center by adhering to facts, owning mistakes quickly, and refusing to let the rapid pace of news distract from investigating the most consequential issues.  
  
### 🌎 Q: How should the U.S. respond to perceived democratic backsliding?  
  
🤖 A: Cobb suggests studying international examples where societies successfully prosecuted corruption or navigated authoritarianism, specifically mentioning Brazil, Italy, and the Philippines.
