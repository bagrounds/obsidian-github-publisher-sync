### ⚙️ Systems Thinking Models  
* 🔄 **Feedback Loops:** Identify and adjust reinforcing/balancing loops to influence outcomes. Understand system behavior.  
* 🚧 **Bottlenecks:** Recognize and leverage constraints to boost innovation and efficiency.  
* 🛡️ **Margin of Safety:** Design for extremes, not averages; ensure buffer capacity for robustness and risk reduction.  
* ⚖️ **Dynamic Equilibrium:** Systems constantly adjust to complex variables; equilibrium isn't always optimal functioning.  
* 🌱 **Emergence:** Understand how simple interactions yield complex, unpredictable system-wide behaviors.  
  
### 🔢 Mathematical Models  
* 📈 **Compounding:** Leverage exponential growth in various domains beyond finance (e.g., knowledge, skills, habits).  
* 📉 **Law of Diminishing Returns:** Recognize where additional input yields decreasing marginal output. Optimize resource allocation.  
* ↩️ **Regression to the Mean:** Expect extreme performance to trend back towards average over time. Avoid misattributing causes.  
* 🤖 **Algorithmic Thinking:** Construct reliable processes for predictable outcomes from given inputs.  
* 📊 **Sampling:** Expand understanding and build accurate conclusions through representative data collection.
