## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🤖 Q: How does Claude’s training differ from ChatGPT?  
  
🤖 A: Claude is built using constitutional AI which follows a set of written principles to guide behavior while ChatGPT primarily uses reinforcement learning from human feedback to maximize user satisfaction.  
  
### 📝 Q: What is the best way to prompt Claude for high quality work?  
  
📝 A: Instead of simple commands, describe the full situation and provide rich context because Claude is optimized to evaluate the framing of a task and provide strategic reasoning.  
  
### 🖥️ Q: What can the Claude Co-work desktop agent actually do?  
  
🖥️ A: It functions as a worker that can autonomously open, read, edit, and organize files in authorized folders on a computer to execute multi-step data management tasks.  
  
### 🚫 Q: What features are missing in Claude compared to OpenAI’s tools?  
  
🚫 A: Users lose access to native image generation, Sora video creation, real-time voice modes, advanced mathematical reasoning, and the custom GPT marketplace.
