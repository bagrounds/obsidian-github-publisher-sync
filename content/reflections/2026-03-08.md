## 🤖🐲 AI Fiction  
👣 The hallway stretches into a dim infinity with doors opening on both sides in perfect rhythmic intervals. 🔦 I press my hand against the cold stone and feel a faint vibration as if the walls themselves are thinking. 🚪 Each room I enter contains exactly one object that looks like it belongs to a life I almost remember. 🧶 I tie a red string to the first handle and step into the second circle of the maze. 🧿 The geometry here does not follow the rules of a sun-lit world. 🕸️ I am not just walking I am being sorted through a lattice of interconnected choices. 🌫️ When I reach the tenth level I realize the string is not behind me it is coming out of my own chest. 🕳️ The deeper I go the more I understand that the exit was never a place but a specific combination of turns.
