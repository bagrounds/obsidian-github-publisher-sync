## 🤔 Evaluation  
  
* 🧪 The American Psychological Association notes that parental stress is a significant public health concern, echoing the Surgeon General's advisory mentioned in the video.  
* 📈 While Dr. Becky promotes internal work, The Gardner Report by the Harvard Graduate School of Education emphasizes that systemic support and community resources are equally vital for reducing parental overwhelm.  
* ⚖️ Exploring the balance between internal mindset shifts and practical behavioral modification techniques could provide a more holistic view of child development.  
  
## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### ❓ Q: What does it mean to be good inside?  
  
🅰️ It means recognizing that a child's fundamental identity is positive even when they exhibit bad behaviors like tantrums or lying \[[11:25](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8IWUaRf4aQY&t=685)].  
  
### ❓ Q: How can parents stop tantrums before they start?  
  
🅰️ Removing the element of surprise by explaining upcoming situations helps children manage their expectations and emotions \[[21:07](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8IWUaRf4aQY&t=1267)].  
  
### ❓ Q: Why does gentle parenting feel so difficult for some?  
  
🅰️ Compassion can feel dangerous to the brain because it is a new and unpracticed response compared to traditional discipline \[[02:07:16](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8IWUaRf4aQY&t=7636)].
