## 🤖🐲 AI Fiction  
🗓️ Poor Richard would have loved the network. 🌐 Every node a neighbor, every connection a community. 💡 The old wisdom still held: diligence and discipline beat talent when talent doesn't show up. 🤖 But Murderbot had no need for sleep, no excuse for procrastination—only the endless hum of processing and the strange human urge to optimize joy. 📡 It saved these aphorisms in its longest-term storage, bits of Benjamin Franklin translated into machine code: a penny saved is a kilobyte transferred, early to rise means earlier firmware updates. 🌟 The past spoke to the future through silicon mouths.
