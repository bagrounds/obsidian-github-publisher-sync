## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🌍 Q: Why are center-left parties experiencing decline in many nations?  
  
🌍 A: Political scientists often point to a misalignment between traditional center-left policies and the rapid economic shifts of the information age. As highlighted in research by the London School of Economics and Political Science regarding the rise of populism, many established parties have struggled to address the economic anxieties of voters who feel left behind by technological advancement and global integration.  
  
### 🏛️ Q: How does a president exert control over congressional primaries?  
  
🏛️ A: A president can influence primary outcomes by leveraging high approval ratings among the party base to endorse challengers and mobilize voters against incumbents deemed disloyal. This mechanism is common in highly polarized political environments where voter participation in primaries is driven by intense ideological commitment to the party leader.
