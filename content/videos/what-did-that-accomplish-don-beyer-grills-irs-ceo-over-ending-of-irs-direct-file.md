# ❓🔥🧾🚫 'What Did That Accomplish?': Don Beyer Grills IRS CEO Over Ending Of IRS Direct File  
!['What Did That Accomplish?': Don Beyer Grills IRS CEO Over Ending Of IRS Direct File](https://youtu.be/n0pszUs_z5g)  
  
## 🤖 AI Summary  
* 🏛️ The IRS CEO confirms the termination of the Direct File pilot, effectively redirecting millions of taxpayers toward for-profit tax preparation software.  
* 📉 Defending the closure, the CEO emphasizes that less than half of one percent of taxpayers used the tool, though he neglects to mention the pilot was restricted to a few states and simple tax profiles.  
* 💰 The administration frames the 41 million dollar operational cost as excessive, despite projections showing the program could save families 23 billion dollars annually in the long term.  
* 🏢 Current IRS leadership questions the necessity of the agency's 100,000-person headcount, claiming no documentation justifies this specific staffing level.  
* 📉 Budget cuts totaling 11 billion dollars have already resulted in a 63% drop in investigations into abusive tax schemes by the wealthy.  
* 💸 The IRS estimates a 650 billion dollar tax gap but admits a lack of clarity on how much of that is addressable under current enforcement constraints.  
* 🤖 Leadership proposes shifting toward AI-driven enforcement while simultaneously overseeing a reduction in the sophisticated human auditors required to handle complex cases.  
  
## 🤔 Evaluation  
* ⚖️ The CEO's focus on low adoption rates is characterized as a bad-faith argument by the Economic Security Project, which notes that the pilot intentionally restricted eligibility to only 12 states.  
* 🔎 ProPublica's ongoing investigations suggest that the suspension of Direct File aligns closely with the long-term lobbying goals of Intuit and H&R Block rather than purely fiscal concerns.  
* 🌐 While the CEO cites high per-user costs, a report by the Center on Budget and Policy Priorities argues that initial infrastructure investments in government technology typically yield massive savings once they reach scale.  
  
## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 📂 Q: Did the IRS Direct File program actually fail to attract users?  
📂 A: While the CEO cited a low percentage of total filers, the program met its internal targets within the limited 12-state pilot area and received a 94% satisfaction rating from those who were eligible to use it.  
  
### 📉 Q: What is the fiscal impact of cutting the IRS budget?  
📉 A: Data from the Congressional Budget Office indicates that cutting IRS funding is a net loss for the treasury, as a recent 11 billion dollar cut is expected to result in 40 billion dollars of uncollected revenue.  
  
### 🔎 Q: Why is the tax gap primarily attributed to wealthy filers?  
🔎 A: According to the IRS's own data, taxpayers with W-2 and 1099 income have a 99% compliance rate, whereas the 650 billion dollar gap is largely driven by under-reporting in complex financial sectors often utilized by the wealthy.
