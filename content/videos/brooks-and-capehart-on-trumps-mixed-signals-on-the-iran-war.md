## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🛢️ Q: How does the closure of the Strait of Hormuz affect the global economy?  
  
📦 A: The closure spikes global oil prices and disrupts supply chains because the strait is a primary transit point for Middle Eastern petroleum exports \[[02:43](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kxHCYqqeORE&t=163)].  
  
### 🎖️ Q: What is the current status of U.S. casualties in the Iran conflict?  
  
🇺🇸 A: There have been over a dozen U.S. troop casualties reported, including six personnel killed during an aircraft refueling crash \[[00:52](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kxHCYqqeORE&t=52)].  
  
### 📜 Q: What is the primary purpose of the SAVE Act?  
  
📝 A: The legislation requires proof of citizenship and stricter photo identification for individuals registering to vote in federal elections \[[08:09](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kxHCYqqeORE&t=489)].
