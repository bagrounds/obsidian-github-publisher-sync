### 🕵️ Q: Why are Homeland Security Investigations agents frustrated with their current assignments?  
  
🕵️ A: Agents are being diverted from high-level criminal investigations like human trafficking and child exploitation to focus on administrative immigration enforcement and mass deportation quotas.  
  
### 📉 Q: How is the current immigration policy affecting ongoing criminal court cases?  
  
📉 A: Aggressive deportation is leading to the dismissal of serious criminal indictments because defendants are being removed from the country before they can be prosecuted in court.  
  
### 🛑 Q: What specific types of crimes are going uninvestigated due to these shifts?  
  
🛑 Q: Criminal cases involving fentanyl smuggling, gang activity, cybercrime, and financial fraud are taking a backseat to processing individuals with old, low-level immigration violations.
