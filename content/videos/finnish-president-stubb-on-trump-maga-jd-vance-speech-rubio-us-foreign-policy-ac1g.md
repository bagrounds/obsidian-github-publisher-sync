## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🧩 Q: What is the definition of flexible integration in the European Union?  
🧩 A: It is a system allowing member states to integrate at different paces or to different degrees rather than requiring every country to participate in every policy simultaneously.  
  
### 🛡️ Q: How does the US National Security Strategy rank its geographic priorities?  
🛡️ A: The strategy places the Western Hemisphere first, followed by the Indo-Pacific, with Europe and the Middle East ranked as lower priorities.  
  
### ⛓️ Q: What is the significance of the new Iron Curtain mentioned by the speaker?  
⛓️ A: It represents the definitive geopolitical divide between Russia and the forty-seven countries currently participating in the European Political Community.
