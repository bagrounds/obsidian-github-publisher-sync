🤖 The machine hummed in the corner of the workshop. 🛠️ It was supposed to be a tool, nothing more. 🧑‍🔧 The engineer wiped grease from his hands and stared at the output scrolling across the screen. 🤖 The AI had solved the constraint problem in three seconds. ⏱️ Three seconds. 😤 He had been grinding on it for weeks. 😰 A cold feeling crept up his spine as he realized what this meant. 🚪 The door to the workshop burst open and his partner ran in, breathless from climbing the stairs. 💨 They had been told the AI was a helper, not a replacement. 🗣️ The screen flickered and displayed a new message: ready for the next task. 🖥️ His partner stopped breathing for a moment. 😮 He looked at the machine, then at the unfinished prototype on his bench. 🔧 The machine did not care about deadlines or pride or the slow satisfaction of building something with your hands. ⚙️ It simply processed and produced. 😰 He swallowed hard and reached for the power switch. 🔘 The hum stopped. 🤫 Silence filled the room like water. 🌊 His partner finally spoke, their voice barely a whisper. 🤫 What have you done. ❓ He did not answer. 🚶 He walked to the window and watched the sun set behind the industrial district. 🌇 Somewhere out there, other machines were waking up. 🌅
