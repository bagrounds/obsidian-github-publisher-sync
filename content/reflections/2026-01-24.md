🕊️🚫 But the Strategy won... and it killed all peace!  
  
🇺🇸🗽 So wake up, you People! Stand tall on your feet!  
📢🥁 Don't let the masked agents patrol every street.  
🛡️🔥 For if you stay silent and wait for the bell,  
🏚️📉 You'll find that your liberty's turned into hell!
