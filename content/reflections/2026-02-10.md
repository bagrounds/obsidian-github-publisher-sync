# 2026-02-10 | 🧽 Absorbent 💪 Strength 📚  
## 🤖💌 AI Poetry  
🧽 The mind is soft, the mind is deep,  
🧠 A sponge that does not need to sleep.  
✨ It drinks the world through open pores,  
🌊 And carries knowledge to its shores.  
  
💪 The strength of few becomes the base,  
🌱 From which the many build their space.  
📐 Each pattern caught, each concept grown,  
🧩 Becomes a brick the child has known.
