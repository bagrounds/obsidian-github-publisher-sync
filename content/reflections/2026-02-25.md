## 🤖🐲 AI Fiction  
📖 The almanac fell open to January. 👴 Marcus read the same line his grandfather had read fifty years ago: Early to bed and early to rise, makes a man healthy, wealthy, and wise.  
  
🔔 His terminal chimed. 📊 The algorithm had finished its analysis: users who engaged before 6 AM showed 40% higher lifetime value.  
  
😂 Marcus laughed, sharp and bitter. 🗣️ You too, huh?  
  
📱 He minimized the report and kept working. 🧠 The old wisdom wasn't wrong. Just incomplete. ➕ Add a server farm and infinite coffee and you got the same result with better odds.
