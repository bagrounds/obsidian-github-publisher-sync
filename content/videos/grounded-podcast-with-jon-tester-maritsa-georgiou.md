## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 💰 Q: How has the cost of running for the US Senate changed over the last two decades?  
🪙 A: Costs have increased tenfold, rising from approximately 27 million dollars in 2006 to over 280 million dollars for similar races in 2024. \[[03:13](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kqDNX5pFa6E&t=193)]  
  
### 📜 Q: What is the Montana Plan for campaign finance reform?  
📜 A: It is a ballot initiative requiring corporations to agree not to donate to political campaigns as a condition of filing to do business within the state. \[[18:25](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kqDNX5pFa6E&t=1105)]  
  
### 🏗️ Q: How does dark money influence the daily operations of Congress?  
🏗️ A: Lawmakers often avoid taking necessary actions or regulating industries because they fear billionaires will spend millions of dollars to unseat them in retaliation. \[[11:43](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kqDNX5pFa6E&t=703)]  
  
### 🚜 Q: Why are rural voters feeling disconnected from traditional democratic institutions?  
🚜 A: Economic pressures like rising input costs for farmers and the feeling that the system is rigged for those with extreme wealth have eroded trust in government. \[[31:03](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kqDNX5pFa6E&t=1863)]
