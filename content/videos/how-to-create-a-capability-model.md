![How to create a capability model](https://youtu.be/C1Te0V1lWjM)  
  
## 🤖 AI Summary  
  
* 🏗️ Capability models provide a holistic perspective for businesses to transform with specific intent. \[[00:09](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C1Te0V1lWjM&t=9)]  
* 🗺️ Managers use these models as heat maps to prioritize resources based on organizational strategy. \[[00:27](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C1Te0V1lWjM&t=27)]  
* 💎 Capabilities form the foundation for delivering customer value through specific business processes. \[[00:53](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C1Te0V1lWjM&t=53)]  
* 🧩 A capability consists of the ability to perform tasks combined with managing relevant information. \[[03:02](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C1Te0V1lWjM&t=182)]  
* 🧱 Complex capabilities should be broken down into granular levels, typically levels one through three. \[[04:23](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C1Te0V1lWjM&t=263)]

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### ❓ Q: What is the primary purpose of a business capability model?  
  
🚀 A: It serves as a fundamental tool to understand an enterprise holistically so leaders can prioritize investments and transform the business based on strategy.  
  
### ❓ Q: How do capabilities differ from business processes?  
  
⚙️ A: Capabilities represent what a business does and the resources it has, while processes represent the sequence of steps taken to deliver value using those capabilities.  
  
### ❓ Q: Why are heat maps used in capability modeling?  
  
🔥 A: Heat maps visually identify which capabilities are critical, which consume the most resources, and which provide a competitive advantage to the firm.  
  
### ❓ Q: What are the levels of granularity in a capability model?  
  
🪜 A: Models are typically organized into levels one, two, and three to provide both a high-level big picture and enough detail for tactical implementation.
