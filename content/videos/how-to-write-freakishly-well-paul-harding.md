# ✍️🤯📝 How to Write Freakishly Well - Paul Harding  
![How to Write Freakishly Well - Paul Harding](https://youtu.be/roLRPQPQU58)  
  
## 🤖 AI Summary  
  
- 📝 Writing is fundamentally an act of improvisation, not adhering to strict outlines.  
- ⌛ Patience is more valuable than speed for creating meaningful work.  
- 🧠 Characters should emerge naturally through the writing process rather than being constructed beforehand.  
- 🎨 Using specific language to describe sensations with botanical precision is essential.  
- 🏆 Winning awards requires continuing to write from an internal aesthetic rather than external pressure.  
- 📚 High quality literature does not rely on plot, but rather the vivid description of life's wonders.  
- 📖 Reading the dictionary enhances vocabulary for precise expression.  
- 🎯 Write for brilliant readers instead of trying to please everyone.  
- ✂️ Revision is a crucial process, often involving cutting a manuscript down to a fraction of its original size.  
  
## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### ❓ Q: Why avoid outlines when writing novels?  
  
❓ A: To allow for emergence, which is a property that arises from complex systems that cannot be predicted beforehand.  
  
### ❓ Q: How can a writer develop a unique style?  
  
❓ A: Writers must slow down, observe sensations pre linguistically, and let their unique artistic sensibilities refract through their brain to produce a distinct fingerprint.  
  
### ❓ Q: What is Paul Harding's approach to revising a manuscript?  
  
❓ A: The revision process involves writing extensive drafts and then chiseling away to distill the work down to its most precise and elegant expression, often reducing a thousand pages to just one hundred fifty.
