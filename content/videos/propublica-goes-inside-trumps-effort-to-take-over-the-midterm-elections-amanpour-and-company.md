## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🧐 Q: What is the Save America Act mentioned in the report?  
🧐 A: The Save America Act is a legislative proposal championed by Donald Trump that mandates voter ID and proof of citizenship for all federal elections.  
  
### 🖥️ Q: How has CISA changed under the current administration?  
🖥️ A: The administration has reassigned over 30 cybersecurity experts and dismantled the team’s mission to monitor election-related disinformation and hacking threats.  
  
### ✉️ Q: How does the new executive order affect mail-in voting?  
✉️ A: The order seeks to give the US Postal Service authority to determine voter eligibility based on federal lists rather than following state-managed lists.
