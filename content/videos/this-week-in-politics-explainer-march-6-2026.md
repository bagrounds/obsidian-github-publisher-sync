### 💳 Q: Why is the Kansas drivers' license law considered a tool for criminalization?  
  
⚖️ A: By invalidating licenses overnight, the state forces individuals to drive on suspended status, creating an immediate legal pretext for fines and arrests.  
  
### 🌐 Q: What is the rules-based international order mentioned in the video?  
  
🤝 A: It is the system established after World War II based on representative democracy, global trade, and international institutions to prevent world-scale conflicts.
