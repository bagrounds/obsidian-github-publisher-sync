## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)  
  
### 🌍 Q: Why is the Senate filibuster viewed as a target for reform by some political leaders?  
  
🌍 A: Critics argue the filibuster originated from a clerical error rather than design and functions as a mechanism for senators to abdicate personal responsibility, obstruct majority-supported legislation, and evade transparency.  
  
### 💻 Q: How do digital platforms influence modern political polarization?  
  
🌍 A: Social media algorithms utilize engagement-based models that prioritize inflammatory content, which accelerates radicalization and fosters ideological silos that erode the ability to engage in civil, cross-partisan discourse.  
  
### 💰 Q: What is the primary concern regarding the intersection of private wealth and political power?  
  
🌍 A: The fusion of private wealth and state power threatens democratic integrity by allowing ultra-wealthy donors and corporations to exert disproportionate influence over elections, lobbying, and policy outcomes, often bypassing the will of the broader electorate.
